The F44 "Retribution" is a multirole fighter. Its design incorporates elements of the infamous F22 "Raptor" and the newly released F35 "Lighting". The F44 features a small fusion reactor, a singular main engine with a 1MN afterburner hooked directly up to the reactor, two counter rotating fully articulated turbofans, and spacefaring capabilities.
